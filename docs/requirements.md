# Bug Tracking System

## User requirements

1. An administrator can add a project, including its name and description.
1. An administrator can add another administrator to the system.
1. A customer/staff member/administrator can open a bug to an existing project.
1. An administrator can add a staff member, including his name, email, and seniority level.
1. An administrator can add/remove a staff member from an existing project.
1. An administrator can assign a staff member to a bug.
1. An administrator can view bugs assigned to a specific staff member.
1. An administrator can view unassigned bugs.
1. A staff member can view bugs assigned to him.
1. A customer/staff member/administrator can view bugs (including their status) of a given project.
1. A staff member/administrator can close a bug as a duplicate, not reproducible, already fixed, or not a bug.
1. Customers, staff members, and administrators have a username and password that they use to login to the system.
